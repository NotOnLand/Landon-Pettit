Cockatails - Bartending for Birdbrains

A cocktail recipe site meant to be as friendly as possible to beginners. I noticed most drink sites have a fancy or downright elitist feel, so I wanted Cockatails to be straightforward and inviting. Users can browse recipes, search by category, or enter what ingredients they have to see what they could make. Each ingredient has a small info popup that leads to a page with more detailed descriptions and examples, and the 'How-To' section gives step-by-step instructions for common actions like shaking and muddling.

My first foray in to Wordpress, recipes and ingredients can be added as custom posts. View a live version here.
