Shirts from SustainU

Since March 2019 I've had an internship at SustainU Clothing, a small design and printing company. We do work for a lot of Appalachia-based events and businesses, as well as Major League Baseball. Most of my work consists of converting designs for various games and teams, but lately I've also worked on the monthly T-Shirt Club. Each month, subscribers get unique themed shirts for their favorite teams.

Working with the MLB has presented a lot of new and unexpected challenges as a designer; heavy style guides and an extremely strict trademarks department make even coming up with feasible ideas difficult. On the other hand, limitation inspires innovation, and I think my designs working here are among my best.
